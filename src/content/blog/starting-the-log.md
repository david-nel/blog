This site is a place for practical notes from programming, technical side projects, and the slow work of improving at a craft.

Some posts will be polished essays. Others will be closer to field notes: a problem, the context around it, the approach that worked, and the mistakes that made the final answer easier to remember.

The goal is simple: write down the details that are worth keeping, and make them available in a form that might save someone else a few hours later.
